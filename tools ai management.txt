Corrected Tools List for AI Camera Monitoring System (No Sensors)
#	Tool	Why You Need It
1	Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W	The brain of your AI camera. Runs the AI analysis on the device itself (edge computing)
2	Camera Module 3 (Wide Angle)	The eye of your system. Captures clear images of elephant grass leaves. Autofocus is critical for sharp images at different distances
3	4G HAT Module (SIM7600G-H)	Sends alerts and photos to your phone via WhatsApp without WiFi. Your farm has mobile network, so this works perfectly
4	4G SIM Card	Provides mobile network connection for the 4G HAT. Use any Jio or Airtel SIM with data plan
5	MicroSD Card (32GB or 64GB)	Stores the operating system, AI software, and captured images
6	Camera Ribbon Cable	Connects the Camera Module to the Raspberry Pi. Usually included with the camera
7	5V Power Supply (3A)	Powers the system during testing on your desk or near farmhouse
